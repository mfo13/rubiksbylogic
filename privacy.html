Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes in a simple and transparent way how the website Rubik's by Logic handles information from its visitors.

Data collected

This website does not require registration, does not request personal data for browsing, and does not maintain a user database.

If the visitor grants consent, only browsing statistics are collected through Google Analytics. This information is used exclusively to understand how the website is used and to identify opportunities for improving its content.

Cookies

Google Analytics uses cookies to generate usage statistics. These cookies are only created after the visitor's consent through the Klaro! consent manager.

Consent can be changed or revoked at any time via the Cookie Settings link available in the footer of all pages on the website.

Feedback form

The website provides a link to a feedback form hosted by Google Forms.

This form is only accessed when the visitor voluntarily clicks the respective link. Filling out the form is optional and subject to Google's privacy policies.

Sharing of information

This website does not sell, trade, or share personal information of its visitors.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect changes in the website's operation or applicable legislation. Users are encouraged to review it periodically.
