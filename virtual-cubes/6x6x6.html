Verdes' Cube
6x6x6

Inventor: Panagiotis Verdes in 2003.

Release: 2008 (Verdes Innovations S.A., Greece)

Possible combinations: ~ 1,57 x 10116 (estimative of iron atoms to fill up the whole observable universe: ~ 3,03 x 10109)

Instructions:
  • To rotate the entire cube in space, simply click and drag on the gray background using your mouse or your finger if you're using a touchscreen device.
  • To rotate specific layers of the cube, click and drag on the facelets of the desired layer in the direction you want to rotate it.
