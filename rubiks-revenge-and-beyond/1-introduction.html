Rubik's Revenge and beyond

Solving Big Cubes by Logic

1. Introduction

The primary objective of this guide is to present a comprehensive solution strategy for solving big cubes, starting from 4x4x4 and beyond, without the need to memorize specific move sequences or algorithms. Instead, we will focus on a simple strategy that utilizes basic moves, such as commutators and conjugates, which are easy to understand and execute with a little practice and skill.

Important Note: This guide assumes prior knowledge of commutators, conjugates, and solving the Rubik's Cube (3x3x3) without relying on pre-memorized move sequences or algorithms. If you are unfamiliar with these concepts, please refer to the guide titled "Understanding and Solving the Rubik's Cube Without Algorithms" before proceeding.

The solution approach presented in this guide aims to minimize the use of complex commutators and conjugates, instead emphasizing the utilization of simple moves like the "go, swap and back" technique. By following a logical sequence of steps, which are easy to remember and visualize, you will be able to solve any NxNxN cube based on the Rubik's cube.
