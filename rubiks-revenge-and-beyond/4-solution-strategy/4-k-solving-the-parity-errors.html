4.k. Resolving parity errors

In some cases, after completing the previous steps, a parity error may occur. While the approach presented in this guide minimizes the chances of encountering parity errors related to the 90ᐤ rotation of the last layer, it's still possible for other types of errors to arise.

To gain a deeper understanding of the origins of parity errors and how to resolve them without relying on specific move algorithms, I highly recommend referring to the guide titled "Understanding the Origin of Parity Errors and Solving by Logic". This comprehensive guide will provide valuable insights and strategies to overcome any remaining parity errors you may encounter during the solving process.
